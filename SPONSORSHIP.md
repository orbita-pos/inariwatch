# Sponsoring InariWatch

InariWatch is 100% Free SaaS and Open Source (CLI). We do not lock features behind paywalls. Instead, we rely on the generosity of the community and corporate sponsors to keep the servers running and development active.

By sponsoring InariWatch, you help us pay for:
- Database hosting (Neon)
- Global edge delivery (Vercel / Cloudflare)
- Email delivery for critical alerts (Resend)
- Continuous development of the open-source Rust CLI

## Sponsorship Tiers

### ☕ Supporter ($5/month)
For individuals who use InariWatch for their personal projects and want to buy the maintainer a coffee.
- A sponsor badge on your GitHub profile
- Immense gratitude from the open-source community

### 🚀 Startup Sponsor ($100/month)
For startups and small teams that rely on InariWatch in production.
- Your company logo (small) on the `README.md`
- Your company name listed on the `SPONSORS.md` file

### 💎 Corporate Partner ($500/month)
For larger companies (like Vercel, Sentry, or Datadog) that integrate with InariWatch or appreciate the value it brings to their ecosystem.
- Your company logo (large) prominently displayed on the **InariWatch Landing Page** (inariwatch.com)
- Your logo at the top of the GitHub `README.md`
- Priority bug fixes and feature requests for the integrations you care about
